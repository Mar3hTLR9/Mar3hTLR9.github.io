My League of Legends

It all began when I was in 10th grade. Back then, the hottest online game was Cross Fire, but due to hack and cheats, the game eventually got shutdown. I started to look for a new game; the first game was Dota. The version that year was only Dota 1, which was not very friendly to new players. They did not have a specific item tree that allowed players to keep track of upgrading items. A lot of my friends recommended League of Legens (LoL), so I gave it a shot. It has been six years since, and I am still playing it.
