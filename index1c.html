Thresh

"The mind is a wondrous thing to tear apart." - Thresh

Thresh is a supporter in LoL. A supporter in LoL protects teamates and mainly controls vision on the map. After watching a few matches of Madlife and Mata, I decided to play Thresh. Among four active abilities, Thresh has three skillshots. It was a proof that I could handle a harder champion. Unlike other supporters, Thresh can be played both offense and defense. His skillshots can open a combat or simply disengage enemies from killing teamates.
